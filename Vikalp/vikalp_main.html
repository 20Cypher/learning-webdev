Vikalp is our solution to the issues people in rural areas face. We give the people a platform to select their issues, after which we provide our accurate answers, gathered by collecting data from multiple sources. If an issue is excessive, we ensure to direct our users to government officials, or a trusted company which can guide them on the right path. Our mission is to use modern technology, which isn't freely available to people in rural areas, to help with their betterment.
